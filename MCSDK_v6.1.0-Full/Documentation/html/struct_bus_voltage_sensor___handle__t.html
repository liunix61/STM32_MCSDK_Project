◆ ConversionFactor

uint16_t BusVoltageSensor_Handle_t::ConversionFactor

It is used to convert bus voltage from u16Volts into real Volts (V). 1 u16Volt = 65536/ConversionFactor Volts For real sensors ConversionFactor it is equal to the product between the expected MCU voltage and the voltage sensing network attenuation. For virtual sensors it must be equal to 500
