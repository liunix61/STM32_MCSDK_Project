◆ hC3

int16_t STO_PLL_Handle_t::hC3

\( F_1 \) in Hertz [Hz].
Max Application Speed in Rotation per minute [rpm].
Motor Bemf Constant in Voltage line to line root mean square per kilo-rpm [Vllrms/krpm].
\( L_s \) in Henry [H].
Max Measurable Current in Ampere [A].
State Observer Execution Rate in Hertz [Hz].

◆ hC4

int16_t STO_PLL_Handle_t::hC4

Can be computed as \( K_2 × Max Measurable Current / (Max Application Speed × Motor Bemf Constant × √2 × F_2 × State Observer Execution Rate) \)
\( K_2 \) being one of the two observer gains.

Motor Bemf Constant in Voltage line to line root mean square per kilo-rpm [Vllrms/krpm].
